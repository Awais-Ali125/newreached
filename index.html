Our Process

We’ve designed an accessible workflow that allows us to meet your teams wherever they are at, guiding them
through our collaboration and ensuring easy communication, stakeholder clarity, and a fantastic result.

  • 01

    Discovery

    Project needs assessment & stakeholder alignment (DNA)

  • 02

    Plan

    Break down project components - coordinate with teams and stakeholders

  • 03

    Design

    Ideate - create look and feel, kick off content creation

  • 04

    Development

    Deliverable production - whether physical, digital or both.

  • 05

    Quality Check

    Stress test - from user and bug testing to measuring outcomes and engagement
